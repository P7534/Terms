Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use BrightAspirations, we collect information you provide directly, such as when you create an account, connect third-party services like TikTok, or publish content through our platform.

  • Account information (name, email, profile details)
  • TikTok account data when you authorize our application
  • Content you upload and publish through our service
  • Usage data including interactions, preferences, and activity logs

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services, including the TikTok content posting integration.

  • To authenticate and manage your TikTok connection
  • To publish content on your behalf via the TikTok Content Posting API
  • To send important notices about your account and our services
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or security issues

3. TikTok Integration

When you connect your TikTok account, we request permission to access your profile information and publish content on your behalf. We only use these permissions for the features you explicitly authorize.

Your TikTok access tokens are stored securely and are never shared with third parties. You can revoke access at any time through your TikTok settings or our dashboard.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • TikTok, solely for the purpose of content publishing you authorize
  • Service providers who assist in our operations under strict confidentiality
  • Law enforcement when required by applicable law

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption at rest and in transit, secure API key management, and regular security audits to protect your data.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. You may also disconnect your TikTok account and revoke all permissions through our platform settings.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at brightaspirationsconsulting@gmail.com
